Our Franchise Models

ICECREAM KIOSK

Kiosks are small, temporary booths placed in areas with high foot traffic that are usually located in malls or concourses.

ICECREAM CAFE

Icecream cafe is where the customer can have icecream as well as other fast food items in a relaxed environment.

ICECREAM PARLOUR

Ice cream parlors are dedicated places that sell ice cream to consumers, offering a wide variety of flavors and toppings.
